User Guide

Getting Started

1. Set up companies
Go to Companies and create your employer/agency profiles. Set pay mode, overtime, and bonus rules.
2. Add vehicles
Use Vehicles page to add registrations, then assign vehicles to each company.
3. Configure defaults
In Companies, set pay, overtime, default start/finish times, leave allowance, and any company-specific rules.
4. Enter shifts
Use Enter Shift for work days, annual leave, sick days, mileage, expenses, and night-out pay.
5. Review summaries
Open Summary for week/month or 4-weekly views, company breakdowns, and PDF export.

Full Guide

Companies
Each company stores rate, overtime multipliers, pay mode (weekly/daily), default times, leave allowance, bonus settings, and field visibility toggles for vehicle/trailers/mileage.
Vehicle Assignment
Vehicles are assigned per company. Enter Shift only shows vehicles assigned to the selected company, but new typed registrations are saved and assigned automatically.
Shift Types and Leave
Day/Night shift flag is stored. Night sets default date to previous day on new entries. Annual Leave and Sick Day are mutually exclusive and counted in yearly stats.
Mileage, Expenses, and Night Out
Mileage is calculated from start and finish mileage when enabled by company. Expenses track parking and tolls. Night-out pay is tracked and included in totals.
Summary and Payslip Export
Summary supports weekly and month panel views, with monthly/4-weekly toggle. Export creates a print-ready summary including pay components and net after expenses.
Backup and Restore
Use the Backup & Restore section at the bottom of All Shifts. Restore automatically migrates older backups to the current data model.
